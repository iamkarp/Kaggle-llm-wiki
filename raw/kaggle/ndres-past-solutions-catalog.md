## Key Patterns Across Solutions

### Most Common Winning Techniques (from code repos)
1. **Entity embeddings** — Rossmann 3rd (pioneered the technique that became standard)
2. **Multi-level stacking** — Crowdflower 1st, Homesite 1st (KazAnova's approach)
3. **Sparse convolutions** — Diabetic Retinopathy 1st, DSB 2017 1st
4. **Feature engineering pipelines** — Click-Through Rate 1st, Avito 1st (Owen Zhang)
5. **Pseudo-labeling + TTA** — Multiple CV competitions
6. **Online learning/inference** — Several time-series competitions

### Competition Types Covered
- **Tabular**: Santander, IEEE Fraud, Elo, Homesite, Avito, Give Me Some Credit
- **Computer Vision**: Clouds, Severstal, Kuzushiji, APTOS, Diabetic Retinopathy, Right Whale
- **Medical Imaging**: RSNA Hemorrhage, SIIM Pneumothorax, Cervical Cancer
- **NLP/Text**: Jigsaw Toxicity, Crowdflower Search, Stack Overflow
- **Audio**: Freesound Audio Tagging
- **Science**: CHAMPS Molecular, Higgs Boson, Predicting Biological Response
- **Object Detection**: Lyft 3D, Open Images
- **Generative**: Generative Dog Images
- **Time Series**: Rossmann, Winton Stock Market
- **Recommendation**: R Package, YouTube-8M
